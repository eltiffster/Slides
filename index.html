Prototyping the Past

1) Resists nostalgia, glorification, or re-enactment

2) May be premised on not replicating history, what not to repeat

3) Tests suspicions by grounding them in matter and fine-grained details

4) Recognizes how many aspects of technology and history exceed our control, access, and understanding
